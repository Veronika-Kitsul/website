Good old (and perhaps a little boring) game, in which you bounce the ball into the tiles above 'in the sky' and try to not lose the ball as it bounces back to the platform it started from. Coded as a junior in high-school.

(*) Kevin Bacon Game

Enter the names of two actors and get a path from one to another by common movies and other actors-colleagues for them. Additionally, see three alternative paths or which actor is the farthest for the first one. Played around with some graph algorithms. Coded as a junior in high-school.
